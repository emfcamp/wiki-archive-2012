Arrival

Getting to the site

Minibus

Hopefully the majority of people will be getting physically to the site via the coachway i.e. using the shuttle bus.

  • Do we want who-evers on the minibus to check tickets?
  • This may be a bottle neck if people bring lots of baggage

Car

Whilst most people should be using the minibus there will be some people with on site parking and some who want to drop things off

  • Need Dropoff area / parking side
  • Need alternative parking location to the coachway (West of Willen Lake is the Peace Pagoda car park, medium sized, not huge)
  • Will need printed parking tickets to issue to people

Ticketing

  1. Verify ticket - proposal is QR code & unique ID emailed to attendees
  2. Get appropriate wrist band (day Vs camp)
  3. Get bag o' Gubbins
    • camp only? or does everyone get a bag but campers get the badge extra

Tent Erection

  1. Lug crap in
    • Are we going to do runs with the Golf cart/Tank to aid this, if so how will it be run, first come first served?
  2. Pitch tent
    • Village areas should already be marked but may need listing somewhere prior so that the villages can find where they should be
    • Need people checking that people aren't in fire lanes etc
    • (What's the deal with the different lettered camping areas?)
      • Initially so we could name the DK's (A1, A2, B1,C1 etc), but also so you could let friends know where your camped. - natm

Get data/power

  1. Attendee leaves cat5 & power by DK
  2. NOC team plug in
    • How regularly will orbits be done?
      • Probably once an hour on Friday, less frequently over the weekend --Davidc (talk) 22:08, 25 August 2012 (UTC)
      • they will be able to SMS or tweet @emfnoc to request this too
    • How are we going to enforce e.g. 'no cables across the fire lanes'?
      • Any staff seeing such a cable should remove it and trace it back and educate whoever's at the other end of it --Davidc (talk) 22:08, 25 August 2012 (UTC)
      • How are we marking the fire lanes? --Davidc (talk) 22:08, 25 August 2012 (UTC) - with survey spray and red/white tape, natm.
  3. Log onto Wifi
    • What are the plans/penalties for DHCP hosting etc?
      • NOC will be monitoring and shut down any ports where people are engaging in such shenanigans. --Davidc (talk) 22:08, 25 August 2012 (UTC)

Talks

Speaker

  1. Turns up at appropriate tent & at appropriate time
    • What do we do about no-shows (when is it classed as a 'no-show')?
    • How much prior to their start time should speakers arrive (5min?)
    • NEED to inform speakers of what they need to do/bring
  2. Plug in anything needed
    • Will we have machines spare for people with USB sticks?
  3. Gives talk
    • What's the policy for trouble makers? Assume stage helper tries to quieten if not get stewards and put on naughty chair / kick out of camp
  4. Questions
  5. Stage helper indicates 5 min left
  6. Stage helper indicates end of talk, next speaker starts set up

Helper

  1. Turns up for shift & signs in @ info desk
    • conditions & response to no-shows?
  2. If new to role gets brief (10 min prior to shift start?)
    • NEED Write briefs
  3. Goes to tent does handover
    1. Check status of equipment,
    2. Tidying
    3. Check schedule for shift
  4. Check-in speaker
    1. Have they arrived
    2. Slides working
    3. Any special requirements
    4. Streaming status?
    5. Get mics set up
  • Do helpers need to limit number of people in a marquee?

Leaving

  • When will people start wanting to leave?
  • When do people need to have left by?
    • We'll start shutting things off at 12:00 on Monday and redirecting all web traffic to the nyancat vm - natm.

Getting from the site

Minibus

  • Is everyone going to want to leave at once?

Car

Whilst most people should be using the minibus there will be some people with on site parking and some who want to pick things up

  • Need Pick up area / parking side

Problems

In General

  1. Contact stewards
  2. Stewards will try to talk situation down
  3. Isolate troublemaker/s
  4. Either cool off at gate or phone police
  5. Write up log (for anything that involves stewards getting called)
    • Usually the controller operator / team leader maintains the log, stewards radio in when they are dealing with issues, they are free to move onto assisting with other issues then - natm

Drunks

  • Bar staff need to know they can are required to cut people off
  • Water at gate/strategic locations
