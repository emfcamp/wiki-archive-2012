Power (Russ/Will)

New progress/issues:

  • Having spoken to many hire companies we are struggling to find suitable towable generators, because they are not that commonly used and those that are have been booked for Olympic related events.
  • Non towable generators will have a larger capacity, but also run quieter due to being event spec.
  • All dry hire power kit will arrive either on pallets or in containers designed to be lifted by crane or telehander/forklift (see - http://yfrog.com/h8d17bjyj and http://foto.eth-0.nl/main.php?g2_itemId=15638)
  • Telehandler rental is £402 for the week (inc vat and delivery/collection from site) from Ace Parts hire in MK. This will also be useful for other purposes. (We would like to avoid the expense but are runnng out of options). Requires trained personnel, £300 per person. Russ proposes he and ??? are trained and cover 50% of training themselves.
  • Will has been exploring options for telehandler training.
  • Also planning to determine whether we can pump out the flooded underpass next to the enatrance for potential access with taller machinery
  • We are hoping to to place orders for generator and distribution kit by July 20th.

Sanitation (Crypt)

  • Currently toilet hire, for 10 x toilets, 1 x disabled, 1 x 5 man urinal and cleaning £1860, this includes the toilets we currently have hired for datenklo, but will increase as we now need more
  • Fencing, waiting for revised quote for lower fencing requirements, although from the last quote I've estimated this at about £1950, they like to send quotes by post though so will wait for this (also keen to have a half-height fence)
  • water: anglian water require lots of paperwork and council permission before doing servey. Looks as if best bet is to go with tankers, estimated cost £6000
    • Also got in touch with Barrel Bikers to get details for standpipe access
  • Showers: 1 x 6 bay shower cost £1530 (incl wastewater removal)
  • flooring: need to confirm exact area we need, in theory this should be reletively cheap ( no more than £300 absolute maximum )
  • Litter: still waiting on final quote will give final numbers on this next week

  • Telehouse router delivered, awaiting correct codes / keys from Pulsant for rack access
  • Getting additional prices on backup wireless link kit.
  • Still waiting on confirmation of SFP loan from Flexoptix HQ

Comms
