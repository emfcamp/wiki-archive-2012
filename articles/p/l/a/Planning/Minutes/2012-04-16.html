Ticket Sales

Ticket pricing policy:

  • are there free tickets for sponsors or speakers?
    • consensus is: no. Everyone will pay for their ticket. (Maybe with some very, very rare exceptions.)
    • we esp don't want people getting free tickets and then selling them

Crowdfunding?

  • do we want to consider crowdfunding? (e.g. sponsume)
  • e.g. for first batch of ticket sales? Or for karma?
  • open question; Russ will be building the payment system, lots will depend on that

Budget

  • total expected expense so far: £50k
  • ~20% of that in next 2-3 months

Pre-sale

  • ~400 email addresses collected so far
  • pre-sale price should be ~50% of ticket price
    • it's likely a non-refundable deposit! ("Best effort to pay back"; e.g. pro-rata payback after deducing fixed expenses)
    • event won't happen without this pre-sale
  • Current proposal: £25 pre-sale
    • we could treat it as a suggested minimum; anything beyond the final ticket price will be considered a donation
    • And we'll open up the budget as part of the pre-sale
  • Let's pick a pre-sale deadline!
    • OPEN: Jonty: We need a list of milestone dates! (On a wiki page)
