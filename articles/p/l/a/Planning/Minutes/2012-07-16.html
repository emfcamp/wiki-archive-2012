Team Updates

Administration / Treasury / Budget (Russ / Nick)

  • TEN was approved, we're surprised that it happened so quickly
  • Account balance: £1,700 (and waiting for an invoice to come through, and potentially more GoCardless payments)
  • Total tickets sold: 128 (outstanding pre-pays plus full tickets)
  • Will send "full tickets now sold" email to pre-sale ticket holders
  • Atm planning for 250 attendees, will adjust infrastructure budgets accordingly
    • But also adding a handful of day tickets (30 slots: 90 tickets), they will cost more than a third of a price
    • Day tickets to go on sale immediately (at £40 a pop)
    • If we end up selling much more we will scale up accordingly (where possible)
  • Insurance broker having a hard time finding event cancellation insurance: concerns that we're on a floodplain
    • Public liability, employer's liability, and property insurance are fine
    • Now looking at an alternative option, and trying to talk to Anglian Water etc
    • Will discuss this again next week. (We don't want to do a bloc.)
