MK Visit

Today's visit by Mark, Jonty, Russ, Jasper, Adrian

Summary:

  • Mailing list thread: Milton Keynes Status
  • We have venue confirmation. More paperwork to come. Managed by the Milton Keynes Parks Trust (MKPT)
  • Everyone's in a great mood now. Jonty is exhausted.
  • Jonty will share photos
  • Russ to email list and tweet to announce date
  • Russ will also produce a map

Terrain

Location:

  • Ordnance Survey Reference for one of the M1 bridges: SP 885 413
  • TODO: geo coordinates, links to Google Earth, maps
  • Details should be shared at Location

Notes:

  • The camp site is surprisingly large
  • M1 traffic louder than expected, but volume drops off quickly
  • Excellent bridges underneath M1; need to find owners.
    • Surprisingly quiet underneath, but occasional bassy rumbles.
    • Gravel ground. Some of it would need to be cleared.
  • Massive open unused fields on the other side of the M1; should identify owners, maybe we can use some of it
  • Lake is a nature reserve: no swimming/boats
    • Nearby lake: details unclear
    • An "abnormal flood" may lead to the lake overflowing ("unlikely 80-year scenario"); the camp site is on a flood plane
    • More likely: ground will be damp/soggy
  • Massive campfire site nearby
  • As mentioned: nearby unused helipad
  • MKPT is concerned about the lasting effects of events; need to plan carefully.

Parking, Caravans

  • Will need to figure out parking. No more than 100 cars will be able to park easily.
  • 2-3 parking sites
    • On at least one of these (for 30-40 cars) the council doesn't charge
    • There's a P&R near Pineham
  • One potential space on the other side of the bridge; would require groundworks
  • Can we make a caravan/camper area? probably won't have the space. Tbd.
  • The camp site won't accommodate cars; that would damage the grass
  • We definitely will charge an additional vehicle cost. Space is too limited.
    • Also note that MK is probably the most train-accessible area in the country.

Logistics

  • Found a power box in field (Jasper has all the details)
    • Mailing list thread: Power On Site
    • Should ask Benny to figure out if we can set it up to reduce dependency on generators
  • Jasper trying to estimate tent density
    • Mailing list thread: Tent Density
  • Contact for "environmental health": Gillian Clark
  • Contact for toilets: Site Plant Services
  • Are we still planning to build a bridge? Probably too expensive, and building ourselves too risky
    • Jasper to research cost

Licensing

  • Contact: Ed Fisher
  • It was pointed out to us: unlikely we'll get a late license
    • For amplified music, alcohol sales after ~11:30
    • We'll have to enforce this (Russ/the company is liable)
    • Rule: if we can hear your music on the camp site after 11:00pm you need to turn it off
  • Russ will apply early and get a first opinion

Teams

Let's start making a list of teams we need, and fill them. But also want to open it up slowly.

  • Suggestion: let's pick three top priorities now and form new teams around them; then repeat
  • Our general approach to this is by iteration: deal with problems as they come, and shape teams accordingly
    • We will try hard not to be too rigid
  • Note that we're building this on a trust system; we'll have to get to know you before we let you take on large responsibilities

Expectations of each team:

  • Stay in touch. Document your plans on the wiki, participate in mailing list discussions.
  • Certain teams *need* to be present at our physical planning meetings (via a representative.)
  • More details tbd.

Existing teams:

  • Internet (Jasper)
  • Site Planning (Russ)
    • Camp layout, maps, etc
