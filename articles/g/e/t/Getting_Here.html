Getting Here

From Electromagnetic Field


The site is located at Pineham Park, in Milton Keynes.

By Bike

Bicycles and motorbikes will be allowed on site for free, and can be kept by tents, but you must wheel them when inside the gate. Enter via the Tongwell Street entrance.

By Train

You should arrive at Milton Keynes station, which is a major stop on the London-Manchester line. The number 300 bus runs from the station to Milton Keynes Coachway about every 15 minutes, and from there you can catch our shuttle service to the site (Friday and Monday), or walk (15 minutes).

By Car

Park & Ride

We recommend you park at Milton Keynes Coachway Park & Ride. This is just down the road from the site, and we'll be running a shuttle service on Friday and Monday. It's also about a 15 minute walk. Parking is free and there's CCTV.

Parking tickets

If you've bought a parking ticket in advance, you'll be able to park on site, outside the fence. Turn up at the Tongwell Street entrance and our staff will direct you. If there's nobody around or the gate is shut, please ring HQ on +44 (0)7449 676467.

The onsite parking is now sold out.

Camper Vans

Camper vans turning up during the event outside the hours of midday-midnight can park on site. We'll be emailing you before the event with more details.
