Remit

  • Find the caters for the event
  • Find bar staff for the event
  • Make sure that the staff working with food have all passed the food safety award level 2

Food Safety Qualifications:

  • http://www.findcourses.co.uk/training/cieh-level-2-award-in-food-safety-103612
  • This would be a single day course and if we all pay attention and do what we are told we will come out with the certificates.
