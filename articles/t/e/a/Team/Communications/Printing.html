Notes

This page is just a quick set of notes on various printing costs (& lead times) for reference.

Based mainly on first google hit.

For paper prints all estimates are for 1,000 prints (i.e. 2x our maximum number of people).

Program

This is probably over kill for what we want/need but worth keeping in mind

A4 8 pages

  • £420 (£595 for heavier cover)
  • page
  • 3-7 days lead time (after receipt of payment & confirmation of proofs) assume 2 week full turnaround
  • Full colour
  • Matt/gloss finish
  • includes delivery

A5 8 Pages

  • £304 (£435 for heavier cover)
  • page
  • 3-7 days lead time (after receipt of payment & confirmation of proofs) assume 2 week full turnaround
  • Full colour
  • Matt/gloss finish
  • includes delivery


Leaflets

A4 Folded

  • £130.00
  • Page
  • 5-7 working day lead time
  • double sided colour print
  • half, letter or zig-zag fold

A4 Leaflet

  • £118.00
  • page
  • 5-7 working day lead time
  • double sided colour print

A5 Leaflet

  • £84.00
  • page
  • 5-7 working day lead time
  • double sided colour print

Posters

UKPS only seems to do runs of 500+ A3 posters (which are here) and cost £145.70

Banners

8ftx4ft PVC

  • £110
  • page
  • Two colour
  • 2 days of final proof (leave 1 week)


4ftx2ft PVC

  • £35
  • page
  • Two colour
  • 2 days of final proof (leave 1 week)

Clothes

Cheap white

  • £462.5 (£1.85 /each 250 minimum order)
  • White
  • One side printing only
  • 7-10 days lead time

Colour

Not done by UKPS but based on the t-shirt printer:

  • Single colour (on any colour) between £4.99 (50+, £249.5 for 50) and £9.99 (1) per t-shirt (
  • Full colour (on white/pastels) between £5.99 (50+, £299.5 for 50) and £9.99 (1) per t-shirt

Hi-viz vests (yellow/orange)

  • £694.5 (for 150, printed front & back)
    • £1.45 [vest] + £1.19 [front print] + £1.99 [back print] per vest (50-249 ordered)
  • page
  • doesn't include delivery
  • 7-11 days lead time

Hi-viz vests (other colours)

  • £1152 (for 150, printed front & back)
    • £4.5 [vest] + 1.19 [front print] +1.99 [back print] per vest (for 25-249 ordered)
  • page
  • Stuart C. suggested that we should consider using non standard colours
    • helps prevent confusion if someone decides to be unofficially helpful and start parking cars etc
    • also makes them a slightly better goody if volunteers are allowed to keep them
    • Emergency Services prefer stewards not to have the same colour as them (we changed all our work ones to blue recently).

Hi-Viz (Copied from volunteers' page)

Suppliers:

  • workwearexpress - £771.00 for 150 with Logo in Large
  • directworkwearonline - Apparently available in Black - go figure? - £855.00 for 150 Printed in Large
    • It's actually cheaper to get 250 printed (as printing is then free): £675
  • internet-workwear
  • A quid each from a pound shop, a cardboard stencil and some paint

Stickers

  • ~£125
  • page
  • 84 x 55mm
  • 2-9 days lead time

Wristbands

Silicone

  • page
  • £375 - £675 (for 500)
  • 10 work days after funds clearing
    • Cheap: single sided embossing (£0.75 each for between 100-999)
    • Expensive: Double sided two colour (£1.35 each for between 100-999)

Fabric

  • page
  • £305 (for 500)
  • Embroidered message
  • 4 week turn around (including proofing and delivery)
  • 500 minimum

Tyvek

(papery things)

  • page
  • £21.98 for box of 500
  • Set designs (no customisation)
  • next-day delivery available

Plastic

(plastic band with hole catch thing)

  • page
  • £35 -£40 (for 500, depending on design)
  • Set designs
  • next day delivery
